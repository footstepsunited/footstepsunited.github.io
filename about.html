About us

Our goal is to remove barriers to equal participation and empower current and future generations of Asian-Canadians through the sport of soccer.

  • We promote soccer to Asian-Canadian communities by specifically targeting young players.
  • We organize an annual soccer tournament for the Asian-Canadian community.
  • We operate a mentorship network amongst Asian-Canadian athletes.
  • We organize conferences and speaking events bringing together the Asian Canadian soccer community.
  • We collect and donate soccer gear and jerseys for youth teams in need.
  • We produce media geared towards Asian-Canadian soccer players.
  • We operate a scholarship fund for Asian-Canadian athletes.

Our stories and our experiences are the reason we started this.

As second generation Asian Canadian immigrants, our co-founders grew up playing house league soccer like most Canadian children. While summers spent on the pitch are some of our fondest memories and likely our athletic primes, we realized that there were very few Asians in these leagues, let alone our own teams. We believe sports, in particular soccer, has the ability to bring people together while having fun. Today, we are fortunate enough to live in todayâ€™s Canada, a melting pot of cultures and ethnicities, but we understand that there is still work to do to promote diversity and inclusion. We hope that our organization can help be a small part of that continued progress.

Our values are the beliefs, philosophies, and principles that drive our work.

  • A commitment to fostering inclusivity
  • A commitment to building a strong Asian community
  • A commitment to the joy/passion for the game of soccer
